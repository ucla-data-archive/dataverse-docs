Dataverse Management
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

A dataverse is a container for datasets (research data, code, documentation, and metadata) and other dataverses, 
which can be setup for individual researchers, departments, journals and organizations.

|image1|


Libra Data users must submit a request to create a Dataverse or sub-dataverse to Libra@virginia.edu
===================================================

If your department, lab, or center would like a dataverse added, please email libra@virginia.edu.
